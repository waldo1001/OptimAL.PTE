OptimAL Business Operations Control Center

This interface provides access to core business operations:

  • Customer Management: Export, reporting, and dashboard functions
  • Analytics & Reporting: Sales analysis and revenue calculations
  • Order Processing: Batch operations and order management
  • Data Management: Test data generation and maintenance

Execute operations to test system performance and identify optimization opportunities.

Ensuring Adequate Test Data

Most performance tests require a minimum of 25,000 records in the Performance Test Customer table to produce meaningful results and demonstrate optimization improvements.

To check your current record count:

View the "Test Data Statistics" section on this page to see how many Customer records exist.

If you have fewer than 25,000 records:

  1. Locate the "Generate More Test Data" action
  2. Click the action to create additional records
  3. The system will generate records in batches until you reach the required minimum
  4. Wait for the generation process to complete (this may take a few minutes)
  5. Verify the record count has increased to at least 25,000

Note: Data generation runs in the background via job queue entries. You can continue working while records are being created.
